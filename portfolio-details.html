Detail Project Information

Interior texture refers to the tactile and visual elements of the surfaces within a room or space. It encompasses the textures of materials used in interior design and decoration, such as walls, floors, ceilings, furniture, and fabrics. Texture plays an important role in creating depth, interest, and visual appeal in a space. By using different textures, designers can add warmth, depth, and dimension to a room, and create a sense of visual interest and richness. For example, smooth and polished surfaces such as marble or glass can create a modern and sleek look.
